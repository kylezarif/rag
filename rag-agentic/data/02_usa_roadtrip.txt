USA Roadtrip Playbook
- Route: cap drives at 5-6 hours/day; alternate drivers; build one buffer day per week.
- Permits: check national park timed entries (e.g., Yosemite), parking reservations, and wildfire closures.
- Car prep: ensure spare tire, jack, coolant; pack jumper cables and a flashlight.
- Safety: avoid overnighting at isolated pullouts; share live location; keep a paper atlas for dead zones.
- Lodging: mix motels and short-term rentals near groceries; book at least 24h ahead in peak season.

Desert & Mountain Driving
- Desert: start at dawn, carry 4L water/person/day, avoid exposed hikes after noon.
- Mountains: watch for altitude effects; downshift on descents; carry chains where required.
- Weather swing kit: light puffer + rain shell even in summer at elevation.

Parks & Trails
- Wildlife: 25 yards from bison/elk, 100 yards from bears; store scented items properly.
- Navigation: offline maps; screenshot trailheads; note sunset times for return windows.
- Leave No Trace: pack out trash; stay on trail; respect quiet hours in campgrounds.

Remote Work & Nomad Tips
- Lodging: confirm desk, seating, and Wi-Fi (ask for speed test); stay near cafes/co-working as backup.
- Power: adapters, spare USB-C cables, compact surge protector; mind voltage.
- Schedule: block meeting times in local + home TZ; communicate async updates.
- Data: SIM/eSIM with hotspot allowance; consider unlimited evening plans for uploads.
- Health: daylight for circadian alignment; short walks between calls; stretch kit in luggage.

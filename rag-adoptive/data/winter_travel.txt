Winter Travel & Snow Destinations
- Layers: moisture-wicking base, insulating mid (fleece/down), waterproof shell; pack glove liners and warm hat.
- Footwear: waterproof boots with tread; add microspikes for icy sidewalks if needed.
- Transit: allow extra transfer time; monitor storm delays for flights/trains; carry a compact scraper and de-icer if driving.
- Safety: watch for black ice; hydrate and moisturize in dry cold; keep batteries warm to preserve charge.

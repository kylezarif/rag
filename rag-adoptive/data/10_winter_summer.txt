Winter & Heat Travel
- Winter: base+mid+shell; waterproof boots with tread; microspikes for icy sidewalks; hydrate and moisturize.
- Driving snow: allow extra time; carry scraper/de-icer; know chain rules.
- Heat: plan outdoors dawn/dusk; hydrate with electrolytes; light, breathable fabrics; UPF hat; sunscreen every 2 hours.
- Lodging: confirm heating/AC; choose shaded rooms or lower floors in hot climates.

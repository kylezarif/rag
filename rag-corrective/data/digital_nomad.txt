Digital Nomad Essentials
- Lodging: confirm desk, ergonomic chair option, and reliable Wi-Fi (request speed tests); stay near cafes/co-working as backup.
- Power: pack outlet adapters, spare USB-C cables, and a compact surge protector; check voltage compatibility.
- SIM/eSIM with hotspot allowance; consider unlimited evening plans for uploads.
- Work hours: align with home base; use time zone converters and clear meeting blocks in calendars.

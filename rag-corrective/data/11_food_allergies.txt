Food & Allergies Abroad
- Carry translated allergy cards; learn key phrases; show staff before ordering.
- Pick eateries with visible kitchens or clear allergen labeling when possible.
- Pack safe snacks for transit days; keep antihistamines/epinephrine accessible.
- Clarify sauces/oils; in some regions peanuts/sesame/soy are common bases.
- Note emergency numbers and nearby clinics; share plan with travel companions.

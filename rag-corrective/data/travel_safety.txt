Travel Safety & Etiquette
- Share itinerary with a trusted contact; set phone SOS and enable device tracking.
- Use hotel safes for passports; carry a photo copy and a backup card separate from wallet.
- At night, stick to lit routes; use licensed taxis or reputable ride-hailing; avoid distracted phone use on streets.
- Etiquette: learn greetings, tipping norms, and dress expectations for religious sites; carry a light scarf for shoulders.

Packing: Carry-On First
- Pack layers: base, mid, light shell; avoid single bulky items.
- Shoes: max two pairs (daily + lightweight dress or trail); pack socks in shoes.
- Toiletries: decant into travel bottles; keep meds in original containers and in personal item.
- Tech: universal adapter with USB-C, compact power strip, and battery pack under airline watt-hour limits.
- Compress with cubes; weigh bag before leaving for strict European/Asian carriers.

Insurance & Safety
- Coverage: trip cancellation/interruption, medical + evac, baggage delay/loss; check adventure exclusions.
- Pre-existing conditions: ensure covered; note primary vs secondary medical.
- Keep policy numbers and insurer contacts saved offline; share with a travel partner.
- Safety habits: share itinerary, enable device tracking, avoid displaying valuables, use hotel safes.
- Night moves: stick to lit routes; use licensed taxis/ride-hail; keep phone use discreet.

Packing Mastery
- Layers: base, mid, shell; avoid single bulky pieces; limit shoes to two pairs (daily + dress/trail).
- Toiletries: decant; meds in originals; carry-on for essentials; backup glasses/contacts.
- Tech: universal adapter with USB-C, compact power strip, sub-100Wh battery pack.
- Organization: packing cubes; roll clothes; weigh bag for strict carriers.
- Special kits: swim + reef-safe sunscreen; cold kit (beanie/gloves); rain kit (shell/umbrella).
